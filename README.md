# Pics Sortz

Pics Sortz is a Flutter application designed to help you organize your photos into groups with ease.

## Features

*   **Group Organization**: Create and manage groups to categorize your photos.
*   **Import Photos**: Easily import photos from your gallery or camera into specific groups.
*   **Cover Images**: Set a representative cover image for each group.
*   **Image Viewing**: View photos in full screen with zoom capabilities.
*   **Edit Photos**:
    *   **Rotate & Crop**: Built-in image editor to rotate and crop your photos.
    *   **Rename**: Rename photos by tapping on the title.
    *   **Delete**: Delete individual photos with confirmation.
    *   **Move**: Move photos between groups easily.
*   **Group Management**:
    *   **Rename Groups**: Rename groups by tapping on the title.
    *   **Delete Groups**: Delete entire groups and their contents.
*   **Persistent Storage**: All data is saved locally using Hive.
